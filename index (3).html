Our Founder’s Story

A Smile That Changed Everything

Our story begins with a smile.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, while working as a chef at a guest house in Jinja District, I had an unexpected encounter that would forever change the course of my life—and plant the seed for Agape Kids’ Empowerment Ability Network.

One afternoon, as I was heading home after my shift, I saw a teenage boy carrying his younger brother on his back. The younger boy had a visible disability, and the care and love in the older brother’s eyes moved me deeply.

This moment made me realize how many children with disabilities in our communities are in need of compassionate care, support, and opportunities to thrive.

Motivated by this encounter, I founded Agape Kids’ Empowerment Ability Network to provide holistic support, empower families, and advocate for inclusion.

Our Programs

We offer a range of impactful programs tailored to the needs of children with disabilities and their families, including:

  • Rehabilitation and Therapy Support
  • Inclusive Education Initiatives
  • Family and Community Empowerment
  • Assistive Technology Provision
  • Income-generating Projects for Families

Our goal is to build sustainable foundations for inclusion and equal opportunity in Kamuli District and beyond.
